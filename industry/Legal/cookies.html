Cookies Policy

How we enhance your browsing experience

1. Why we use Cookies

We use cookies to keep your session active. Without these, our "Google Login" and "Download Gatekeeper" wouldn't know who you are as you move between pages.

2. Types of Cookies

  • Authentication: Keeps you signed in while browsing.
  • Preferences: Remembers your search filters and view settings.
  • Analytics: Helps us see which assets are the most popular.

3. Your Choices

You can clear your cookies via your browser settings at any time. However, logging out of Microassets will automatically clear your active session cookie.
